# 📊 Regional Daily Sales Monitoring Dashboard

## 📌 Background
This project is designed as a **daily sales monitoring dashboard** for Regional X. It provides continuous visibility into revenue performance and transaction activity, allowing stakeholders to monitor business performance on a daily basis **throughout the year until year-end closing**.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Monitor **daily revenue trends continuously**
- Track transaction activity in real time  
- Compare year-over-year performance (2025 vs 2026)  
- Identify fluctuations and anomalies in daily performance  
- Support daily operational monitoring and decision-making  

---

## 🛠 Tools
- Power BI  
- Excel  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** 2839.83 Billion  
- **Total Transactions:** 1.12 Billion  

### Yearly Breakdown:
- **2025 Revenue:** 1439.99bn | Transactions: 650.50M  
- **2026 Revenue:** 1399.83bn | Transactions: 470.05M  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

### 📉 1. Continuous monitoring reveals declining trend in 2026
- Revenue decreased from **1439.99bn (2025) to 1399.83bn (2026)**  
- Transactions dropped significantly from **650M to 470M**  

👉 Ongoing monitoring helps detect performance decline early before year-end.

---

### 📊 2. Daily revenue shows high volatility
- Revenue fluctuates significantly across days  
- Both upward spikes and sudden drops are observed  

👉 Indicates unstable daily performance requiring closer operational control.

---

### ⚠️ 3. Daily anomalies highlight potential issues
- Several days show noticeable revenue dips compared to surrounding periods  

👉 These anomalies may indicate:
- Operational issues  
- Campaign performance changes  
- External factors affecting sales  

---

### 📅 4. Gradual decline observed over time
- Monthly view shows relatively stable patterns  
- However, 2026 consistently underperforms 2025  

👉 Indicates a structural performance issue rather than isolated events.

---

### 🌐 5. Revenue heavily depends on broadband services
- Broadband contributes **~2377bn**, dominating other service types  

👉 High dependency on a single service creates potential business risk.

---

### 🏷️ 6. Revenue concentration in one brand
- Brand A contributes **~99.42% of total revenue**  

👉 Indicates very high reliance on a single brand.

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations  
- Use continuous daily monitoring to quickly detect and respond to performance issues  
- Investigate anomalies in daily revenue drops  
- Implement strategies to stabilize daily revenue patterns  
- Diversify revenue streams beyond broadband services  
- Reduce dependency on a single brand to mitigate risk
