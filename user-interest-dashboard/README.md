# 📊 Regional Customer Behavior & Interest Profile

## 📌 Background
This project analyzes customer behavior and digital platform preferences in Regional X. The objective is to understand how users interact with different types of services such as music, video, social media, games, and shopping platforms to support data-driven marketing and product strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify dominant digital platforms across categories
- Analyze customer preferences and penetration rates
- Understand user distribution across different services
- Support targeted marketing and partnership strategies

---

## 🛠 Tools
- Power BI
- Excel

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Music Users:** 1.62M  
- **Video Users:** 2.68M  
- **Chat Users:** 2.83M  
- **Social Media Users:** 3.48M  
- **Games Users:** 2.99M  
- **Shopping Users:** 2.47M  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 📱 1. Social media has the highest user base
- Social media users: **3.48M (highest among all categories)**
  
👉 Indicates strong engagement in social platforms, making it a key channel for marketing campaigns.

---

### 💬 2. Chat platforms show extremely high dominance
- WhatsApp dominates with **86.15% penetration**
- Other platforms (Telegram, Discord) have minimal usage

👉 Suggests a highly concentrated communication channel ecosystem.

---

### 🎵 3. Spotify leads the music streaming market
- Spotify: **47.68% penetration**
- YouTube Music: ~28%

👉 Shows strong user preference toward a single dominant platform.

---

### 🎥 4. Video consumption is concentrated on YouTube & TikTok
- YouTube: **51.74%**
- TikTok: **39.87%**
- Other platforms (Netflix, Vidio) have very low share

👉 Indicates strong dominance of free/short-form video platforms.

---

### 🛒 5. E-commerce usage is led by Shopee
- Shopee: **58.92%**
- Lazada: 24.18%
- Tokopedia: relatively low share

👉 Highlights market leader dominance in online shopping behavior.

---

### 🎮 6. Gaming market is highly fragmented but dominated by “Others”
- Others category: **84.48%**
- Top games (Mobile Legends, Roblox) have smaller shares

👉 Suggests user interest spread across many games rather than a single dominant title.

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations
- Prioritize **social media and chat platforms** for marketing campaigns  
- Focus partnerships on dominant platforms such as **WhatsApp, YouTube, Spotify, and Shopee**  
- Explore opportunities in **video content and short-form media (TikTok, YouTube)**  
- Investigate deeper into gaming preferences due to fragmented behavior  
- Develop targeted promotions for high-engagement platforms
