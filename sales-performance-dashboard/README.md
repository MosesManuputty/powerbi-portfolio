# 📊 Regional Sales Performance & Revenue Analytics

## 📌 Background
This project aims to analyze sales performance in Regional X to support business decision-making at the end of 2025. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of revenue distribution, customer segments, and regional performance to identify growth opportunities and potential risks.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Monitor overall revenue performance
- Analyze customer contribution and transaction activity
- Identify high-performing and underperforming regions
- Understand revenue distribution by customer segment, device, and tenure

---

## 🛠 Tools
- Power BI
- Excel

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** 16.63 Billion  
- **Paying Subscribers:** 159K  
- **Total Transactions:** 242K  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔥 1. Revenue is highly driven by long-tenure customers
- Customers with **Length of Stay >12 months generate ~13.8bn (~83%) of total revenue**
  
👉 Long-term customers are the primary revenue drivers, highlighting the importance of retention strategies.

---

### 🌍 2. Sales contribution varies significantly across clusters
- Cluster A: 1.90bn  
- Cluster D: 1.53bn  
- Cluster B & C contribute significantly less  

👉 Indicates uneven regional performance and opportunity for targeted improvement.

---

### 🏙️ 3. Large gap between top and bottom cities
- Top city revenue: 1.53bn  
- Lowest city revenue: 0.008bn  

👉 Suggests strong performance concentration in certain cities and underperformance in others.

---

### 📱 4. Android dominates revenue contribution
- Android contributes **~88% (14.66bn)** of total revenue  

👉 Business strategies should prioritize the Android user segment.

---

### 💎 5. GOLD customers are the main revenue contributors
- GOLD tier contributes **8.66bn (~52%)**
- Other tiers (Regular, Platinum) contribute less  

👉 High-value customers play a critical role in revenue generation.

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations
- Focus on **customer retention strategies**, especially for long-tenure users  
- Improve performance in lower-performing clusters (Cluster B & C)  
- Conduct deeper analysis on underperforming cities  
- Optimize product and marketing strategies for Android users  
- Strengthen loyalty programs for high-value (GOLD) customers
